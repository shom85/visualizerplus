The Newest 3D Kitchen From Visualizer Plus: Tuscan Milano

Welcome to the Tuscan Milano kitchen, which showcases a fusion of traditional and modern design. Here, you can swap out every feature and appliance until you create the kitchen of your dreams. Choose from over 16 billion combinations by selecting from various countertop, countertop edge, cabinet, cabinet handles, flooring, wall color, appliance, and lighting options. You’ll be able to build your kitchen using products from the top kitchen companies in the world, such as Viking, Marble.com, Feiss, Cubitac, Mr. Stone, Wolf, Lumber Liquidators, and DiMonte. Once you’ve found an item that you like, click the tag to find where to buy it. After finding a design that you’re happy with, save, print, email or share it.

Countertops

Perimeter countertops should be chosen with longevity in mind. Materials such as granite and quartz will be able to stand up to the daily wear and tear of cooking, entertaining, and cleaning. Some softer materials like marble and quartzite can be used too, but will require additional maintenance to preserve their beauty. Marble.com has provided the largest selection of granite colors in the industry.

Kitchen islands can be designed to either match the perimeter countertops or be used as an accent piece. Using a contrasting color can create an attractive design, whereas keeping the colors consistent creates a cohesive décor.

Cabinets

Cabinets are typically one of the first elements decided on for the kitchen. Cubitac and Hampshire have provided a great selection of dark and light cabinets including handles for you to choose from. White cabinets have become a popular kitchen design trend because it makes the room to appear more spacious. Espresso cabinets are a great option too. They will for provide a warm and cozy kitchen environment.

Backsplash

Kitchen backsplashes no longer simply protect walls from spills and splatters, a wide array of eye-catching materials like granite, travertine, and other natural stones can be used to make the backsplash the focal point of today's kitchens. The full backsplash in this kitchen is an excellent way to add some unique style to the hub of your home.

Countertop Edge

The fine details of your kitchen come together to create a unique décor. Although, selecting an edge for your countertops may seem like an afterthought, it will add the perfect finishing touch. Using a straight or quarter round edge will fit a modern design, whereas, edges such as Ogee and Bullnose works best for traditional decors.

Floor

Floors are going to take a beating anywhere, but especially in the kitchen. Selecting a kitchen floor that looks great but is also durable and stain concealing is important. Selecting from our great variety of options from Lumber Liquidators and Ann Sacks, you’ll be able to find the hardwood or tile floor that is right for your home.

Chairs

The breakfast bar chairs are from Bue Dot and are available in 10 different colors to suit your specific kitchen design.

Stove

The stove is the hub of kitchen activity. Stainless steel is durable enough to handle the wear and tear that a heavily used stove will experience over years of cooking. Select from Viking and Wolf, two of the most popular stainless steel stove companies on the market today.

Oven

Having a separate oven from the stove is a popular and practical design that allows for ease of preparation when making large meals for entertaining. The oven that you see here is a part of the Viking line. You can also select from Bertazzoni ovens as well.

Range

The stove range, or hood, seen in this kitchen is 48 inches wide, which on the larger side. There are a variety of options available from Viking and Wolfe, some of the top range companies. Generally, the range will match with the appearance of the stove, however, it’s your decision to keep it consistent or change things up.

Lighting

Lighting is one of the most important elements of any room. The amount of natural light your room receives will determine what kind of lighting is best for your kitchen. Choose from a variety of Feiss fixtures to determine what style fits best for your design.

Wall Color

The wall color for your kitchen sets the mood for the room. Choose from a vast selection of Behr paints to find the tone that best suits yours. Lighter colors will be ideal for smaller rooms or those are short on natural light, whereas, if natural light is plentiful in your kitchen, warmer colors can look great.

Refrigerator

The refrigerator is one of the most important components of any kitchen. Not only will keep your food fresh, but it is an essential design point. Stainless steel refrigerators are a sanitary and stylish option. Choose your ideal fridge from our selection of line of Viking.

Other Appliances

Some additional appliances in this kitchen are a state-of-the-art wine fridge and dishwasher. Options for each of these are provided by Viking and feature beautiful stainless steel options that are durable and stylish.

Sinks

The sink is the workhorse of the kitchen. It endures the daily wear and tear of cooking and cleaning, therefore requiring a durable but, of course, stylish option. Choose from the great line of DiMonte sinks available here to find the right one for your kitchen design.
